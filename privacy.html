Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

📋 Our Commitment: At Government Job Hai, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide Directly

When you use our website, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile Information: Qualification, location, preferences
  • Job Application Data: Jobs you've saved or applied to
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, or feedback you send us

b) Information Automatically Collected

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Log Information: IP address, access times, referring URLs
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: For analytics and functionality

c) Third-Party Information

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as:

  • Google Analytics (for website analytics)
  • Firebase Analytics (for user behavior tracking)
  • Google AdSense (for displaying ads)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Personalization: To show relevant job recommendations
  • Communication: To send job alerts, updates, and responses
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve our website
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdSense

3. Data Storage & Security

🔒 Security Measures:
  • Firebase (Google Cloud): User accounts and authentication data stored on Firebase Free Tier
  • Encryption: Passwords are hashed and encrypted
  • Access Control: Limited access to personal data
  • Regular Backups: Data is backed up regularly
  • SSL/TLS: All data transmission is encrypted

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality (login, sessions)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics and Firebase Analytics to understand user behavior
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense to show personalized ads
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and preferences
⚠️ Cookie Control: You can control cookie settings through your browser. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Services & Partners

We use third-party services that may collect and process your information:

a) Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: User authentication, database, analytics
  • Data: Email, password (hashed), profile data, usage patterns
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

b) Google Analytics

  • Purpose: Website analytics and user behavior tracking
  • Data: IP address (anonymized), pages visited, device info
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

c) Google AdSense

  • Purpose: Display advertisements and earn revenue
  • Data: Cookies, browsing behavior for ad personalization
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Ad Settings: Google Ad Settings

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Server Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security purposes

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
💡 How to Exercise Your Rights: Send an email to support@governmentjobhai.com with your request from your registered email address.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Third-Party Website Links

Our website contains links to external government and third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external websites you visit.

10. Google AdSense & DoubleClick Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google Ad Settings
  • NAI Opt-Out: Alternatively, opt out through the Network Advertising Initiative

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes (if you have an account)

12. Compliance with Laws

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • GDPR (for European users)
  • CCPA (for California residents)

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@governmentjobhai.com
  • Website: Contact Form
  • Response Time: We will respond within 30 days
⚠️ Important: By using Government Job Hai, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website.
